Week 2 Assignment - convey a digital message

Make a little research on current societal issues: famine, human rights, democracy, global warming, green life preservation… Based on your mini research, your group will create artistic intervention to convey your message and statement by means of digital art. You want people to become aware of this societal issue. You can decide what will you make, for example: photographs, videos, posters, infographics, drawings and any other format of digital arts…
